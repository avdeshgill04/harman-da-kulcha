# Make Harman da Kulcha website LIVE

Your site is just 3 files: `index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`. You can put it online for **free** in a few minutes.

---

## Option 1: Netlify Drop (fastest — no signup to try)

1. Go to **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
2. **Drag and drop** your whole folder **"Harman Da kulcha"** (the one that contains `index.html`) onto the page.
3. Netlify will give you a **live link** like `https://random-name-123.netlify.app`
4. (Optional) Sign up for a free Netlify account to **claim** the site and get a custom name like `harman-da-kulcha.netlify.app`

---

## Option 2: GitHub Pages (free, custom URL possible)

1. Create a **GitHub** account if you don’t have one: https://github.com
2. Create a **new repository** (e.g. `harman-da-kulcha`), set it to **Public**.
3. **Upload** these files into the repo:
   - `index.html`
   - `styles.css`
   - `script.js`
4. Go to **Settings → Pages** in the repo.
5. Under **Source**, choose **Deploy from a branch**.
6. Select branch **main**, folder **/ (root)**, then **Save**.
7. In 1–2 minutes your site will be at:  
   `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/harman-da-kulcha`

---

## Option 3: Vercel (free)

1. Go to **https://vercel.com** and sign up (e.g. with GitHub).
2. Click **Add New → Project**.
3. **Import** your project (upload the folder or connect a Git repo).
4. Click **Deploy**. You’ll get a link like `https://harman-da-kulcha.vercel.app`.

---

## After it’s live

- Share the link on WhatsApp, Instagram, or with friends.
- Update the link in your social media or business profiles.
- To use your **own domain** (e.g. `www.harmandakulcha.com`), you can connect it in Netlify/Vercel/GitHub Pages settings (they have guides).

**Easiest start:** Use **Netlify Drop** (Option 1), then sign up to keep and rename your site.
